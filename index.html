Speaking Tips

Use Strategic Pauses

Replace filler words with brief pauses. This gives you time to think and makes your speech more impactful.

Control Your Pace

Speak at a moderate pace - not too fast, not too slow. Aim for 150-160 words per minute.

Maintain Eye Contact

Practice speaking while maintaining eye contact with your audience to build connection and confidence.

Practice Regularly

Consistent practice is key to improvement. Record yourself regularly to track your progress.
